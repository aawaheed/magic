# Page Archetype; AI Product Homepage
WORKFLOW ==> page-archetype-ai-product-homepage

## Purpose
A homepage for an AI product that balances futuristic appeal with credibility and clarity.

## Typical section order
1. Hero with clear product promise
2. Trusted by or proof bar
3. What the AI does
4. Workflow or how it works
5. Interactive preview or product scene
6. Key use cases
7. Security, trust, or compliance section
8. Testimonials or case studies
9. FAQ
10. Final CTA

## Hero structure
- Short high-impact headline
- One sentence on what the AI does
- Primary CTA
- Secondary CTA
- Product UI preview, 3D scene, or intelligent motion scene

## Content priorities
- Be specific about outcomes
- Reduce AI vagueness
- Show what the product actually does
- Build trust with clarity and proof
- Keep visual sophistication high but readable

## Common components
- Product demo panel
- Use case grid
- Workflow section
- Security block
- Animated scene or screenshot stack

## Layout notes
- Use strong contrast and clear hierarchy
- Keep support copy tight
- Put proof before deep explanation
- Pair futuristic design with practical messaging

## Do
- explain outcomes plainly
- show concrete workflows
- use premium motion carefully
- include trust signals

## Avoid
- buzzword overload
- abstract messaging only
- excessive effects without product clarity
- burying the CTA
