# UI Design Workflow Breakdown — Plumbing Services Business Landing Page
WORKFLOW ==> design-profile-business-landing-page

## 1) Design Summary
- Style: bold, clean, modern, service-focused.
- Layout: strong hero, service highlights, trust sections, testimonial CTA, contact form.
- Visual tone: bright yellow brand accents, white space, high clarity, approachable professionalism.
- Goal: build trust quickly and convert visitors into booked service requests.

## 2) Typography
- Use a bold sans-serif for headlines and a readable sans-serif for body copy.
- Headings: large, heavy, high contrast, short line lengths.
- Body copy: concise, supportive, easy to scan.
- CTA text: direct and action-oriented.

## 3) Color Direction
- Primary palette: yellow, white, black, and soft gray.
- Accent use: dark navy or charcoal for contrast and depth.
- Yellow should dominate hero, buttons, and key callouts.
- Keep supporting tones minimal to maintain a clean service brand.

## 4) Layout Structure
- Top navigation with logo, links, and a highlighted booking button.
- Hero section with headline, service copy, CTA, and technician imagery.
- Service intro block with image, short description, and feature icons.
- Mid-page value proposition section with supporting text and image.
- Testimonial and quote section with a lead capture form.
- Footer or final CTA zone for conversion closure.

## 5) Components
- Navigation bar with logo, links, and primary booking CTA.
- Hero banner with service headline and support copy.
- Feature icons for speed, emergency support, and reliability.
- Image/text split sections to explain benefits.
- Testimonial block with quote styling and credibility cues.
- Contact or quote form with clear fields and submit button.

## 6) Imagery Direction
- Photography style: real technicians, studio-clean or bright home service scenes.
- Background treatment: light interiors, warm daylight, clean surfaces.
- Composition: active service moments, tool handling, sink and plumbing close-ups.
- Prompt style: “professional plumber in bright modern kitchen, natural light, clean service branding”.

## 7) Design System Notes
- Keep spacing generous for readability and trust.
- Use rounded cards and buttons for a friendly modern feel.
- Shadows should stay soft and minimal.
- Maintain strong contrast for accessibility and clarity.
- Keep CTA placement consistent across sections.

## 8) Follow-Up Upgrade Suggestions
- Add sticky booking CTA for quick conversions.
- Include service area selector or zip-code checker.
- Add urgency features like same-day booking or emergency support badges.
- Use trust signals such as reviews, certifications, and warranty icons.
- Add a FAQ accordion for common plumbing questions.
- Optimize mobile flow with tappable calls and compressed forms.
